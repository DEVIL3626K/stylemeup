COTTAGECORE

Introduce earthy tones for a whimsical wardrobe


The cottagecore aesthetic revolves around the farm core or the fairy aesthetic, thus it is always close to nature. wearing earthy toned tunic dresses, sweaters, checkered dresses gives a sense of freshness and calmness to your style.This aesthetic is a super go to vacation outfit.

Implement your soft side with slit cut soft floral dresses


These dresses are an essential element of cotagecore clothing. Floral patterns are extremely pleasing to the eyes and can be incorporated to those styles which have a certain minimalism in them. Whether its a picnic date or beach brunch with your collegues this always gives a sense of comfort in all events.

Minimalistic jewellery makes a great statement


Minimalist is a type of 'barely there jewellery', thats worn subtle rather than as a bold statement. Its elegant aesthetic complements ll sorts of outfits especially cottagecore ones.
